  Description: library for easy creation of resizable, font-sensitve GUI's
        Notes: This library contains a collection of classes that enable you to
               easily create fully resizable and font-sensitve GUI's.
               Lots of classes are provided (some based on Be's classes, some
               custom classes), and it is very easy to make your own.
               To install, unzip the archive and move liblayout.so to 
               /boot/home/config/lib
